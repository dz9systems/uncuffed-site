IMPACT

Uncuffed has offered groundbreaking prison radio training inside prisons for over a decade. Our participants develop tools build skills for a purpose: to change the narrative about people in prison and to gain job skills.

We’ve worked with over 80 participants, and aired over 140 of their stories and shows. To our knowledge, we have a 0% recidivism rate. No Uncuffed participant has ever returned to prison left prison and gone back.

Uncuffed alumni include the founders of the podcast Ear Hustle, award-winning film documentarians, and many others who have gone on to careers in media, communications, and advocacy.
